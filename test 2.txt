Chhaava is a 1957 Indian Bollywood film directed by Prakash Arora. The movie stars Dev Anand and Kalpana Kartik in the lead roles. Chhaava is a romantic comedy that tells the story of a young man who falls in love with a beautiful woman, but their social differences create obstacles in their relationship. The film features memorable songs and a lighthearted tone, which were characteristic of many Bollywood movies from that era. Chhaava is considered a classic of Hindi cinema and is still remembered fondly by many fans of old Bollywood films.
